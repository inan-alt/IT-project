The One Who Laughs

Posted : Dec 10, 2025

We cannot talk about Batman without talking about Joker who is the arch nemesis of Batman. Their relationship is very interesting as they need one another to survive. This was spectacularly highlighted in the graphic novel, Batman: The Killing Joke, where we see for the first time Batman laugh at a joke by the Joker for the first time, fitting the name "Killing Joke".

Their rivalry is one to watch, as it unfolds through psychological nuances with two troubled individuals which raises some though provoking scenarios. I absolutely adore this and I will till my last day. I am forever grateful that I had the privielage to read through the comics at a young age so that I could exposed to such beautiful yet mature stories.
